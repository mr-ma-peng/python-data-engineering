From EverybodyWiki Bios & Wiki
Jump to:navigation, search

The 100 Films Ranked Highest on Average are The Godfather, Citizen Kane, and Casablanca according to an average ranking of five poll-based and/or expert top 100 lists (Rotten Tomatoes, IMDB, AFI, Empire, and Sight and Sound). The culmination of these sources take into consideration thousands of opinions ranging from expert cinephiles to amateur move-goers.
